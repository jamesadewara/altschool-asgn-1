Vegetable Egusi Stew

A tasty and hearty Nigerian stew made with ground melon seeds, assorted vegetables, and spices.

Recipe Information

Prep time: 20 minutes

Cook time: 40 minutes

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ground egusi (melon seeds)
  • 1 cup assorted chopped vegetables (spinach, ugu, or kale)
  • 1/2 cup palm oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cups meat or fish stock
  • 2 teaspoons ground crayfish
  • 2 fresh peppers, chopped
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the palm oil in a pot over medium heat until hot but not smoking.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Stir in the ground egusi and fry for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
  4. Add the meat or fish stock, then mix in the chopped vegetables and peppers.
  5. Cook on low heat for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add salt and crayfish to taste.

Tips

Use fresh vegetables for the best flavor. You can substitute palm oil with vegetable oil if preferred.
